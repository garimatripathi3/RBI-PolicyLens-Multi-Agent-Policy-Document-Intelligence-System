# Sample Health Insurance Policy (Synthetic)

Effective date: 2025-01-01. This policy is a synthetic example for testing PolicyLens.

## Coverage
The annual coverage limit is $500,000 per insured member. The deductible is $1,500 per
individual and $3,000 per family per policy year. After the deductible is met, the plan
covers 80% of eligible in-network expenses; the member is responsible for the remaining 20%.

## Out-of-network
Out-of-network care is covered at 60% after a separate deductible of $3,000. Emergency
services are always covered at the in-network rate regardless of provider.

## Exclusions
Cosmetic procedures are excluded unless medically necessary following an accident.
Experimental treatments are not covered. Pre-existing conditions are covered after a
waiting period of 90 days from the effective date.

## Claims
Claims must be submitted within 180 days of service. Reimbursement is issued within 30
days of an approved claim. Appeals of a denied claim must be filed within 60 days.
